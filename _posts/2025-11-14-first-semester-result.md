My first semester result was disappointing for me.

My GPA was below 3, and that made me realize I needed to improve much more. I was not satisfied with that result because I knew I could do better.

At the same time, I did not want to see it as the end of my journey. I chose to treat it as a lesson instead of a failure.

This result showed me that I needed stronger concepts, better discipline, and more focused effort in my studies.

## What This Result Taught Me

- I needed more improvement
- I had to study with better planning
- Disappointment can become motivation

## My Response

Instead of stopping, I decided to work harder in the coming semesters.
