My second semester started with a stronger mindset.

At the beginning of this semester, I decided to work harder and stay more consistent with my studies. I wanted to improve myself and perform better than before.

At the same time, difficulties also increased. As the semester moved forward, it became harder to stay fully focused because different pressures started affecting study routine and concentration.

Another major challenge came because of international conflicts and wars. Due to that situation, our classes shifted to online mode. This change affected my learning badly because online classes reduced my understanding and made the study experience less effective.

Even with all these challenges, I did not stop trying. In the final week before mids, I worked very hard, revised seriously, and gave my best effort.

## Main Points of My Second Semester

- I started the semester with more effort and consistency
- Difficulties increased and focus became harder
- Classes shifted to online mode due to international conflicts
- Online learning reduced understanding
- I worked hard before mids and gave my best

## My Reflection

This semester taught me that hard work becomes even more important when conditions are not ideal. Even if the environment is difficult, I still want to keep pushing forward.
