I belong to Shorkot, and moving to Faisalabad for my studies was a major step in my life.

Before this, I had always lived close to home and family. This was my first experience of living in a different city for education, and it felt both exciting and difficult.

Everything around me was new. The city, the people, the routine, and the responsibilities all required adjustment. In the beginning, I felt uncomfortable, but slowly I started understanding how to manage myself better.

This move helped me become more independent. It taught me how to handle daily life with more maturity and how to stay focused on my goals.

## What This Change Taught Me

- How to adapt to a new environment
- How to manage myself away from home
- How to become more independent

## Reflection

Moving from Shorkot to Faisalabad was not easy, but it was an important part of my growth.
