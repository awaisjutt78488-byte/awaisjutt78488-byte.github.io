Hostel life was completely new and difficult for me.

When I first started living in the hostel, I faced many challenges. The routine was different, the environment was unfamiliar, and daily life required much more self-management than before.

In the beginning, adjustment was hard. Small things like time management, study routine, meals, and rest all became challenges. I had to learn how to manage my day without depending on family support.

With time, I became better at handling things. Hostel life taught me discipline, patience, and the importance of managing myself properly.

## Main Challenges

- Adjusting to a new environment
- Building a healthy daily routine
- Staying focused on studies

## What I Gained

Hostel life made me stronger and helped me learn discipline and self-management.
