My first semester subjects gave me a completely new academic experience.

I studied Python and an introduction to Machine Learning along with my other university subjects. For me, these topics were very new, and the workload felt high from the start.

Managing multiple subjects together was not easy. Each course needed time, attention, and regular effort. I had to understand not only the concepts but also how to balance study time across all classes.

Although the workload was heavy, this semester gave me an important foundation for the future.

## What Made It Challenging

- New technical subjects
- High academic workload
- Need for better time management

## What It Gave Me

It introduced me to the kind of serious study and discipline required in engineering.
