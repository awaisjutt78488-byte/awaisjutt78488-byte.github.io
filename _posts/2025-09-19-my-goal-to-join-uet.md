From the beginning, my target was to get admission in the UET main campus.

I worked hard with that dream in mind and hoped to continue my studies there. However, due to preference issues, I got admission in the Faisalabad campus instead.

At first, this was not what I had planned. Still, I decided that complaining would not help me move forward. I chose to focus on improving myself wherever I was.

I believe that success does not depend only on place. It also depends on mindset, effort, and discipline. That is why I accepted the situation and made a fresh commitment to work harder.

## What I Learned

- Sometimes life does not follow our first plan
- Complaining wastes energy
- Growth starts when we accept reality and keep moving

## My Decision

I decided to focus on learning, improving my skills, and making the most of my time at university.
