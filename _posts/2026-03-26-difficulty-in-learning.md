Learning was not easy for me in the beginning.

Programming logic felt hard to understand, especially when I tried to solve problems on my own. At the same time, machine learning concepts seemed very complex and difficult to connect with real understanding.

There were moments when I felt slow and confused. Sometimes I needed more time than expected to understand even simple ideas.

Still, I did not quit. I kept trying, even when progress felt small.

## What Was Difficult

- Understanding programming logic
- Following machine learning concepts
- Staying confident during slow progress

## What Helped Me

Patience and repeated practice helped me continue learning even during difficult days.
